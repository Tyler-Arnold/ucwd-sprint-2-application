Metallica guitarist knocks $3M off Sea Cliff mansion

Metallica guitarist Kirk Hammett’s sprawling four-bed, four-and-a-half-bath hideout at 308 Sea Cliff, listed again last week, this time asking $13 million for the circa 1922 abode. It last sold for a frantic $5.7 million in 2005. (About $7.3 million after inflation.) That’s quite an appreciation in 12 years but, sad but true, it’s also $3 million less than the beyond magnetic property asked a year ago after a sequence of price cuts. Still, when you have at least even odds on an eight-figure home sale in the near future probably nothing else matters. Link
