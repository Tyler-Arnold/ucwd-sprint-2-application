Who are we?

GuitarHub, and our Dorset-based GuitarClub, is an organisation that's dedicated to creating resources to inspire and assist the next generation of guitar players.

Our local club, GuitarClub, brings together Dorset's guitar enthusiasts via a multitude of events. We help bring guitar players to local bands, endorse guitar teachers, and sponsor events and gigs for our local community
